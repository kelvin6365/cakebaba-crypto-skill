## What is the 2B Rule?

The **2B Rule** identifies **false breakouts** (假突破) or **false breakdowns** (假跌破) where price quickly breaks through a key support or resistance level but then rapidly reverses and closes back within the prior range.

### Core Concept:

**"First they fool you, then they take your money."**

- Price breaks support/resistance (traders get trapped)
- Quick reversal occurs (false move is revealed)
- Price closes back inside range (trap confirmed)
- Smart money wins, retail traders get stopped out

              │

                   Price reversed and closed below it
                   This is a 2B pattern = Short signal

## Trading Rules

### ✅ Valid 2B Setup Requirements

1. **Must occur at KEY support or resistance zone**
   - Well-defined horizontal S/R level
   - Tested multiple times preferred
   - Not random S/R levels

2. **Quick Reversal Required**
   - Breakout/breakdown reverses within ~5 minutes (on 5M chart)
   - The faster the reversal, the stronger the signal
   - Slow reversal = weak or invalid 2B

3. **Close Back Inside Range**
   - Candle must CLOSE back inside the prior range
   - If it closes outside, 2B is invalid

4. **Stop Loss Placement**
   - Just beyond the false breakout/breakdown point
   - No need for extra buffer in large markets (BTC/ETH)
   - Tight stops = better R:R ratio

5. **Multi-Top/Bottom Structure** (Optional but Preferred)
   - Double/triple top/bottom more reliable
   - Single top/bottom has lower reliability
   - Confirms the S/R zone is significant

### ❌ Invalid 2B Scenarios

| Scenario | Why Invalid |
|----------|--------------|
| No clear S/R zone | Location undefined |
| Slow reversal (>10-15 min) | Not a quick trap |
| Close outside range | Confirmed breakout, not false |
| Strong trend continuation | Momentum too strong |
| During low liquidity | Spread/slippage risk |

## Risk Management

### Stop Loss Strategy

```
For Bullish 2B (Long):
    Stop: Below the false breakdown low
    Buffer: 1-2 ticks maximum (tight is better)

For Bearish 2B (Short):
    Stop: Above the false breakout high
    Buffer: 1-2 ticks maximum (tight is better)

Why Tight Stops?
• 2B is a trap pattern
• If real move, price should reverse QUICKLY
• Tight stops preserve R:R ratio
• If hit, small loss, next trade
```

### Take Profit Targets

```
Conservative: Closest support/resistance zone
Aggressive: 1:1 risk-reward or better
Trend Trade: If 2B confirms larger reversal

Recommended:
• Target 1: Next S/R zone (take 50% profit)
• Target 2: Extension move if momentum strong
• Move stop to breakeven after Target 1
```

## Common Mistakes

### ❌ What NOT to Do

1. **Trading 2B outside key S/R zones**
   - Random breakouts are not 2B patterns
   - Must be at significant levels

2. **Entering before candle closes**
   - Pattern not confirmed
   - May be genuine breakout

3. **Using wide stops**
   - Defeats the purpose of 2B trading
   - Reduces R:R ratio significantly

4. **Trading 2B in strong trends**
   - Trend continuations override 2B signals
   - Wait for trend exhaustion

5. **Ignoring reversal speed**
   - Slow reversals are weak signals
   - Quick reversals are strong traps

6. **Forgetting to take partial profits**
   - 2B moves can be sharp but short
   - Lock in profits when available

## Trading Psychology

### Understanding the 2B Trap

**Why 2B Works:**

1. **Retail Traders Get Trapped**:
   - Breakout traders enter on momentum
   - Stop losses are placed just outside S/R
   - Smart money knows these levels

2. **Stop Hunt Occurs**:
   - Price pushes just enough to trigger stops
   - Retail traders get stopped out
   - Smart money enters opposite position

3. **Reversal Confirms**:
   - Price moves back in original direction
   - Trapped traders panic (FOMO/fear)
   - Smart money profits from the move

### Left-Side vs Right-Side with 2B

**Left-Side (Before Confirmation):**
- Place orders at S/R zones anticipating 2B
- Better entries but may not trigger
- Requires alerts and patience

**Right-Side (After Confirmation):**
- Wait for 2B + engulfing confirmation
- Worse entries but higher reliability
- Safer for beginners
